XdHacks Mini at Regeneron ISEF 2022

XdHacks Mini will be heading to the 2022 Regeneron International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF) taking place in Atlanta, Georgia, between May 7 and 13. XdHacks Mini is partnering with ISEF to come together for a day of exciting events, activities, and workshops. The theme will be climate change, as this is undoubtedly the most pressing issue that youth face today.

About XdHacks Mini

Founded in 2019, as XdHacks Mini, the non-profit started in Vancouver, BC as a university high-school event. After running a successful event at UBC, XdHacks Mini transitioned to the high-school scene to foster and encourage youth to take up STEM in high school. Over the past 3 years, XdHacks Mini has developed in the premier high-school hackathon organization in Canada and internationally, with deep roots throughout the technology sphere. The organization boasts over 50 high-school students running their own events in their own cities
